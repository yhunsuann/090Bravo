Game Detail
Merge Block Puzzle is a simple but challenging game that will bring you a fun and relaxing time. It's an updated version of classic 2048 number merge puzzle. You can train your mind, and still have fun with this numbered game.


​


FEATURES
- Strategy-first game for sharpening your mind and level up yourself.
- New style of design for number cube blocks.
- No time limit. You can play anytime you like
- Featured with many helpful booster
- Available offline. You can play without Wifi.
- Relaxing music and sound effect to chill your mind
- Simple yet brain-challenging game for all ages.


​


HOW TO PLAY
- You will start with a board filled with some numbered cube blocks
- Simply drag and merge blocks of the same number to create new blocks with higher number. For example, you merge two blocks of 4 to create a new block of 8, two blocks of 8 to create a block of 16, and so on to infinite.
- The more blocks you can merge, the higher your score in this 2048 game
- Don't let the blocks touch the top of the board
- Strategize every move because you can't go back
- Use booster to break your own record.

If you are looking for a fun and challenging merge number puzzle game, what are you waiting for? Merge Block: 2048 Puzzle is a great choice for you
