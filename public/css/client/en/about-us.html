Biography
Sonat Game Studio officially came into operation in 2017 and quickly became one of the potential game developing companies in Vietnam. Until now, the number of those subcribing and downloading Sonat games has reached hunreds of millions of people around the world.
Achievement
The year 2021 marks a breakthrough of Sonat Game Studio developments when Sort Water Puzzle - Color Game reached TOP 1 on Puzzle Game charts in Japan, Korea, Germany and continuously remained top for 2 months. In addition, tens of Sonat products are being available on two application markets which is Google Play and App Store. They have also achieved hundreds of millions of downloads worldwide.
